🎯 Problem Statement

📋 BNH 2026 — ISRO Challenge
Optimizing Urban Heat Mitigation and Cooling Strategies via Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AIML)

Urban Heat Islands (UHIs) represent one of the most critical environmental challenges facing modern cities. THERMOS AI addresses this by combining satellite intelligence, multi-source data processing, and AI/ML models to detect, analyze, predict, and optimize urban heat mitigation strategies.

Unlike existing tools that only map heat, THERMOS AI goes further — explaining heat drivers, simulating cooling interventions, and recommending optimal mitigation strategies with estimated impact metrics.

💡 Innovation & Competitive Advantage

🔍
Differentiation
Goes beyond heat detection — explains drivers, simulates interventions, and recommends optimal strategies with quantified impact.
🤖
Problem-Solving Approach
Integrates satellite, weather, land-use, and urban infrastructure data with AI/ML to identify hotspots, analyze causes, and recommend interventions.
🎯
Unique Selling Point
Single decision-support platform combining satellite intelligence, AI-driven analysis, and what-if simulations for data-driven urban cooling decisions.
