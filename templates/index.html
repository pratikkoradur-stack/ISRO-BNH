Satellite

Multi-Source Satellite Telemetry

We ingest high-resolution raw imagery from Landsat 8 (Thermal Infrared Sensors), Sentinel-2 (Multispectral Instruments), and ECOSTRESS spaceborne radiometers to continuously monitor Bengaluru's surface heat emissions.

🛰️
Detection

AI-Powered Hotspot Recognition

Geospatial convolutional neural networks parse the thermal bands to highlight Urban Heat Island (UHI) zones. Bengaluru's hotspots are automatically isolated and cataloged by severity.

Analysis

Explainable Drivers & NDVI

SHAP values map exact local factors contributing to heat retention. We correlate land cover (NDVI vegetation index), building materials, albedo levels, and wind corridors to understand *why* a hotspot exists.

Prediction

LSTM Temporal Heat Forecasting

Using weather forecasting models (ERA5) combined with long short-term memory (LSTM) networks, THERMOS AI predicts future risk expansion zones over the next 12 to 24 months, indicating critical escalation points.
